How much does it cost to travel to Kaunas?

You should plan to spend around $102 (€) per day on your vacation in Kaunas. This is the average daily price based on the expenses of other visitors.

Past travelers have spent, on average for one day:

  • $40 (€37) on meals
  • $18 (€17) on local transportation
  • $60 (€55) on hotels
